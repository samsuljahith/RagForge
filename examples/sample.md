# Refund Policy

Our refund policy allows returns within 30 days of purchase. Customers must
provide a receipt. Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

## Eligibility

To be eligible, items must be unused and in original packaging. Digital goods
are non-refundable once downloaded.

## Fee Table

| Item type | Restocking fee | Window |
|-----------|----------------|--------|
| Electronics | 15% | 14 days |
| Clothing | 0% | 30 days |
| Software | non-refundable | n/a |
